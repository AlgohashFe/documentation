1.  🔧 打開 SpiderCard 應用，點擊右上角的設置圖標。
2.  🔒 進入設置頁面，點擊**"二步驗證"**(2FA)選項。

![設定 2FA 安全碼指南3](https://milways.gitbook.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F576526375-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FWk98YWM2MHtAzSHwvK6L%252Fuploads%252Fn68i8Mi9c2ljQ1sj1Ja7%252F377.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D7630db9b-9a5c-45c6-9646-469319a114c0&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=bddfb6db&sv=2)
![設定 2FA 安全碼指南4](https://milways.gitbook.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F576526375-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FWk98YWM2MHtAzSHwvK6L%252Fuploads%252Farn5YbUoakZ4dNgYtavT%252F378.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Da3e575e3-d857-4921-9068-ee951775e11c&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=6032c144&sv=2)

3.  📋 在顯示二維碼的頁面，長按代碼並點擊**"複製"**。

![設定 2FA 安全碼指南5](https://milways.gitbook.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F576526375-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FWk98YWM2MHtAzSHwvK6L%252Fuploads%252FlZNPTjPudmXDDH04YuOB%252F379.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D58ec6d27-d238-434a-bed5-05742923a448&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=aa5ac331&sv=2)
![設定 2FA 安全碼指南6](https://milways.gitbook.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F576526375-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FWk98YWM2MHtAzSHwvK6L%252Fuploads%252Fa37qVPuAUbTcTln5GNKe%252F380.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Dea29b399-56e7-480d-b84f-7a230be5fd79&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=eb407c12&sv=2)

4.  📱 打開 Google Authenticator 應用或任何身份驗證器APP，點擊**"添加代碼"**。
    📝 輸入帳戶資料:
    帳戶名稱：Spidercard
    將複製的密鑰粘貼到"密鑰"欄中。

![設定 2FA 安全碼指南7](https://milways.gitbook.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F576526375-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FWk98YWM2MHtAzSHwvK6L%252Fuploads%252FKfoEBnutyzvbmSLc87X0%252F381.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Df4eebb89-1f2a-4f23-ad82-528aa5da1b06&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=b36dc19f&sv=2)
![設定 2FA 安全碼指南8](https://milways.gitbook.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F576526375-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FWk98YWM2MHtAzSHwvK6L%252Fuploads%252FPyiEVV0KDXMMmccV2Xwr%252F382.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Df70efd97-b679-4ba6-9325-430f08747521&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=7cabd4e9&sv=2)

5.  🔢 生成驗證碼
    Google Authenticator 會生成一個6位數的驗證碼（例如：100 075）。

![設定 2FA 安全碼指南9](https://milways.gitbook.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F576526375-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FWk98YWM2MHtAzSHwvK6L%252Fuploads%252FV7lWclfsOIWG77LtM2cu%252F383.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3De01aed6e-7689-4406-a9e7-479b6bfb35ae&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=b51eadf&sv=2)
![設定 2FA 安全碼指南10](https://milways.gitbook.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F576526375-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FWk98YWM2MHtAzSHwvK6L%252Fuploads%252Fe7UvbYAj6kpzBHQOjy2I%252F384.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D9fe9134a-d36d-4837-b2bd-1754e5ae53a4&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=ad6caa97&sv=2)

6.  📝 返回 Spider 應用:
    返回 Spider 應用，使用**"粘貼"或"自動填充"**選項，輸入驗證密鑰，然後輸入 Google Authenticator 生成的6位數驗證碼。
7.  🟢 啟用 2FA
    點擊**"啟用 2FA"**按鈕，成功啟用後界面底部會顯示"已啟用 2FA"的綠色標識。

![設定 2FA 安全碼指南12](https://milways.gitbook.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F576526375-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FWk98YWM2MHtAzSHwvK6L%252Fuploads%252FsTQOd4ZZ2UpIqng9RTap%252F386.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Df72e5791-ff27-4d5a-ba92-527a6a4150dc&width=400&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=13d38747&sv=2)

8.  📧 確認郵件
    您將收到 SpiderCard 發送的確認郵件。
