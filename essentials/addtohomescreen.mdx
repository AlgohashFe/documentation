---

<Info>
To access the SpiderCard platform more quickly and conveniently, you can add it as an app icon on your phone's home screen. This way, you can open SpiderCard directly by tapping the icon, without having to enter the URL through a browser each time. Here are the steps for common mobile operating systems.
</Info>
