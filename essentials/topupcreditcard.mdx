<Info>
Top up your SpiderCard credit limit to enable spending anywhere, anytime. This process involves using USDT collateral from your wallet to obtain card credit limit. Follow these steps to complete the process.
</Info>

## Enter Amount and Get Quote

![Recharging Credit Card 3](https://milways.gitbook.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F576526375-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FWk98YWM2MHtAzSHwvK6L%252Fuploads%252F2kfLtUwIfJzZBdeDiFqW%252F69.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D398f2be7-31a7-4330-aeda-45b53ca0e3e4&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=627d9527&sv=2)

3. ðŸ’µ In the **"Pay USDT"** field, enter the amount of USDT you want to use to obtain credit card limit. This is the amount you'll be using as collateral.

![Recharging Credit Card 4](https://milways.gitbook.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F576526375-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FWk98YWM2MHtAzSHwvK6L%252Fuploads%252FgE32i4oQibfel83eFdFS%252F70.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D6684f669-388e-4b94-8da4-77fbcc5e904b&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=bad835cf&sv=2)

4. ðŸ’µ After entering the amount, tap the **"Quote"** button. The system will calculate the credit card limit (e.g., in HKD) you can receive based on current exchange rates and fees. This uses SpiderCard's RFQ (Request for Quote) feature to provide you with real-time exchange pricing.

## Confirm and Complete Top-up

![Recharging Credit Card 5](https://milways.gitbook.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F576526375-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FWk98YWM2MHtAzSHwvK6L%252Fuploads%252F2PkBFwSnwr1ZrZg6mK9P%252F71.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Dc8ebb2f1-4044-4ea4-82f2-a8fd6de2d350&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=c99f874d&sv=2)

![Recharging Credit Card 6](https://milways.gitbook.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F576526375-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FWk98YWM2MHtAzSHwvK6L%252Fuploads%252FDwFCyeTyhAGD8SSnqLnt%252F72.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D303a4842-2c75-43d3-9a9b-b59571cb7bd1&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=660c08fd&sv=2)
