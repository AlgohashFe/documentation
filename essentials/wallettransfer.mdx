<Info>
SpiderCard 平台允許用戶之間快速、安全地進行內部錢包轉賬。無論是向朋友付款，還是轉移資金，都可以通過 Spider UID 輕鬆完成。本指南將引導您完成轉賬流程。
</Info>

## 發起轉賬

![SpiderCard 錢包轉賬指南1](https://milways.gitbook.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F576526375-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FWk98YWM2MHtAzSHwvK6L%252Fuploads%252FPI2nrrZ4Bevb7SJzbC1D%252F350.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D0cb6206a-14ec-43a9-89a5-e851e20b596a&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=922a4a95&sv=2)
![SpiderCard 錢包轉賬指南2](https://milways.gitbook.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F576526375-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FWk98YWM2MHtAzSHwvK6L%252Fuploads%252F2tICmCDPkgFCMdTvazfc%252F351.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Dbd8e5436-63d4-4428-870f-026eba361af2&width=400&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=15082d0e&sv=2)

1.  👆 在應用程式主畫面，點擊**「轉移」**按鈕。
2.  🔄 在轉賬界面，確保**「至」**欄位選擇的是**「Spider UID」**。這是進行平台內部用戶轉賬的選項。

## 輸入轉賬詳情

![SpiderCard 錢包轉賬指南3](https://milways.gitbook.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F576526375-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FWk98YWM2MHtAzSHwvK6L%252Fuploads%252FSXVpIhgqFjzFWp1Sy28v%252F352.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Dc3296a77-19a3-499b-af75-765742419979&width=400&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=5a7a2c18&sv=2)
![SpiderCard 錢包轉賬指南4](https://milways.gitbook.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F576526375-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FWk98YWM2MHtAzSHwvK6L%252Fuploads%252FAsulBI0xLjpqxk5XEeRa%252F353.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D0fe4b329-9736-4d08-9078-a93cb861fd9f&width=400&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=d4f5eb66&sv=2)

3.  💱 **選擇幣種**: 從「選擇硬幣」下拉選單中，選擇您想要轉賬的貨幣，例如 HKD 或 USDT。
4.  🆔 **輸入收款方 UID**: 在「轉移到 Spider UID」欄位中，準確輸入收款方的 Spider 用戶 ID (UID)。您可以向您的朋友或收款方索取他們的 UID。

<Warning>請務必仔細核對收款方的 Spider UID，輸入錯誤可能導致資金轉移到錯誤的賬戶且無法追回。</Warning>

![SpiderCard 錢包轉賬指南5](https://milways.gitbook.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F576526375-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FWk98YWM2MHtAzSHwvK6L%252Fuploads%252FT8orvoWJtkhtJpfEgdsd%252F354.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D3eaf986e-e260-4afb-8f48-7519fefd0016&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=34f73e05&sv=2)
![SpiderCard 錢包轉賬指南6](https://milways.gitbook.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F576526375-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FWk98YWM2MHtAzSHwvK6L%252Fuploads%252Fas9d3eLmBMSmCvv4k6gS%252F355.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Dd78dbe89-2b3a-4932-9404-235b9e9d2297&width=400&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=79964ac0&sv=2)

5.  🔢 **輸入金額**: 輸入您想要轉賬的具體金額。
6.  🔐 **安全驗證**: 輸入您的兩步驗證 (2FA) 驗證碼以授權交易。這是保護您賬戶安全的重要步驟。如果您尚未設定 2FA，請參考 [設定 2FA 安全碼](set2fa.mdx) 指南。

## 確認並完成轉賬

![SpiderCard 錢包轉賬指南7](https://milways.gitbook.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F576526375-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FWk98YWM2MHtAzSHwvK6L%252Fuploads%252FPrmc9qYNJolOBnGAuxo8%252F356.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D6e6c26fb-609c-40be-b7a9-8a96d44c2ed8&width=400&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=b350bc50&sv=2)
![SpiderCard 錢包轉賬指南8](https://milways.gitbook.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F576526375-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FWk98YWM2MHtAzSHwvK6L%252Fuploads%252FBtjkrWhDD1mj1LD0sa6H%252F357.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D2766a4af-a614-487a-9fc2-f8a1d0a6e131&width=400&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=3840e6f9&sv=2)

7.  ✅ **執行轉賬**: 在確認所有信息（收款方 UID、幣種、金額）無誤後，點擊畫面底部的**「轉移」**按鈕。
8.  🎉 **轉賬成功**: 系統處理完畢後，您將看到**「成功轉移」**的提示信息。資金已即時轉入收款方的 SpiderCard 錢包。

<Tip>SpiderCard 平台內的 UID 轉賬通常是即時到賬且免手續費的，非常方便快捷。</Tip>
