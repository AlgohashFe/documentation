<Info>
SpiderCard platform enables quick and secure wallet transfers between users. Whether you're paying a friend or moving funds, you can easily complete the transaction using Spider UID. This guide will walk you through the transfer process.
</Info>

![Transferring Credit 4](https://milways.gitbook.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F576526375-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FWk98YWM2MHtAzSHwvK6L%252Fuploads%252FAsulBI0xLjpqxk5XEeRa%252F353.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D0fe4b329-9736-4d08-9078-a93cb861fd9f&width=400&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=d4f5eb66&sv=2)

3. üí± **Select Currency**: From the "Select Coin" dropdown menu, choose which currency you want to transfer (HKD or USDT).
4. üÜî **Enter Recipient's UID**: In the "Transfer to Spider UID" field, accurately enter the recipient's Spider User ID (UID). You can ask your friend or the recipient for their UID.

<Warning>Please carefully verify the recipient's Spider UID. Entering an incorrect UID may result in funds being transferred to the wrong account and cannot be recovered.</Warning>

![Transferring Credit 6](https://milways.gitbook.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F576526375-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FWk98YWM2MHtAzSHwvK6L%252Fuploads%252Fas9d3eLmBMSmCvv4k6gS%252F355.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Dd78dbe89-2b3a-4932-9404-235b9e9d2297&width=400&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=79964ac0&sv=2)

5. üî¢ **Enter Amount**: Input the specific amount you wish to transfer.
6. üîê **Security Verification**: Enter your Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) code to authorize the transaction. This is a crucial step for protecting your account security. If you haven't set up 2FA yet, please refer to the [Setting Up 2FA Security Code](set2fa.mdx) guide.
