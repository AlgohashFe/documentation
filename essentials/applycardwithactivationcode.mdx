<Info>
If you have received a Spider physical card with an activation code, this guide will walk you through the process of activating it in the application.
</Info>

<Tip>Please note: You must have your physical card and the provided activation code on hand to complete this process.</Tip>

## Starting the Activation Process

![Activating Card 1](https://www.gitbook.com/cdn-cgi/image/dpr=2,width=760,onerror=redirect,format=auto/https%3A%2F%2Ffiles.gitbook.com%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FBdfmzoYxBtV1Xo4FcaHt%252Fuploads%252FzmXexPW0BXnbwgqUAAwl%252F112.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Dee63314e-4572-435a-9508-14ba1428be95&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=c12560bb&sv=2)

1. üè¶ **Access the Credit Card Section**: Click the "Credit Card" option at the top of the page.
2. üÜï **Activate the Card**: On the new page, click the "Activate Card" button to begin the activation process.

![Activating Card 2](https://www.gitbook.com/cdn-cgi/image/dpr=2,width=760,onerror=redirect,format=auto/https%3A%2F%2Ffiles.gitbook.com%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FBdfmzoYxBtV1Xo4FcaHt%252Fuploads%252F7loIxQkCsxnAMfnpKjTH%252F113.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Dfbb9d66b-b66b-4874-9e0e-b376fd2d5cc0&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=d0416954&sv=2)

3. üíµ **Select Currency**: In the currency selection interface, choose your preferred currency from the drop-down menu.
4. üåç **Select Your Country**: In the Country selection interface, choose your location from the drop-down menu.

![Activating Card 3](https://www.gitbook.com/cdn-cgi/image/dpr=2,width=760,onerror=redirect,format=auto/https%3A%2F%2Ffiles.gitbook.com%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FBdfmzoYxBtV1Xo4FcaHt%252Fuploads%252FMBGET0Rnc5D31tMv1MxG%252F114.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3De50c848d-c3fc-45cd-a8c1-e0c2b0beec47&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=a129a3ff&sv=2)

5. üè† **Enter Card Shipping Address**: Enter the complete and accurate mailing address in the address field.
6. ‚úÖ **Confirm Card Receipt Status**: Check this box to confirm that you have already received your physical card.

<Warning>Please ensure you provide an accurate mailing address if you have not yet received the card. If you have received it, confirm the details match.</Warning>

![Activating Card 4](https://www.gitbook.com/cdn-cgi/image/dpr=2,width=760,onerror=redirect,format=auto/https%3A%2F%2Ffiles.gitbook.com%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FBdfmzoYxBtV1Xo4FcaHt%252Fuploads%252F4ZCNFh0ICIOb7DS0IcBf%252F115.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D2bec5396-3568-4891-bd80-ecd0e9928b07&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=78310bc9&sv=2)

7. üì≤ **Enter Customer Number**: Input your 16-digit customer number, which can be found on your physical card, into the "Customer Number" field.
8. üîë **Enter Activation Code**: Input the unique activation code that was provided with your physical card.

![Activating Card 5](https://www.gitbook.com/cdn-cgi/image/dpr=2,width=760,onerror=redirect,format=auto/https%3A%2F%2Ffiles.gitbook.com%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FBdfmzoYxBtV1Xo4FcaHt%252Fuploads%252FXZNk5RxxywU4RlakUFYk%252F116.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D9f14b67e-6fbc-4d5d-83f7-9e7e049b7dd3&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=cba3b1b&sv=2)

9. ‚úÖ **Complete Activation**: Tap the "Pay and Activate" button to finalize your card activation.

<Tip>
* The application fee is 200 USDT.
* The system supports multiple currencies, including USDT and HKD.
* After activation, the interface may show an available credit limit of HK$0.00 until funds are loaded.
* It is recommended to double-check all entered information to ensure successful activation.
</Tip>
