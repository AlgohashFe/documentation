<Info>
In addition to virtual cards, SpiderCard offers physical card options that you can use at offline merchants and ATMs that accept bank cards. This guide will explain how to apply for your SpiderCard physical card.
</Info>

<Tip>Please note: Applying for a physical card may require meeting specific conditions (e.g., account balance, KYC level, etc.) and may incur related fees. Please check the latest official information for details.</Tip>
